About OK Corrals

Your trusted source for high-quality structures, barns, and shade.

 
   

Our Mission

At OK Corrals, we are dedicated to providing safe and secure enclosures for pets and livestock. Our shade, barns, and corrals are designed to protect animals from predators while ensuring they have ample space to thrive.

Our Story

Founded with a passion for animals, OK Corrals was created to offer peace of mind. We understand the challenges of keeping animals safe in both rural and urban environments, and our products reflect our commitment to quality and security.

OK Corrals was first established in Arizona and launched online in 2006 with the goal of providing high-quality horse shade, livestock fencing, riding arenas, mare motels, animal feeders, stalls, steel sheds, livestock shade, pipe corrals, dog runs, kennels, pipe fences, steel fences, bird aviaries, cattle fencing, and steel gates installation. Our company was founded to address the lack of accessible quality horse shade installation in Southern Arizona.

Every day, we build custom pipe corrals, chutes, stalls, barns, and steel fencing, all of which can be ordered online. We take pride in offering the lowest prices on welding repair, horse corrals, and pipe corrals in Arizona livestock corrals for sale, horse arenas in Arizona for sale, Phoenix portable horse corrals, and wholesale corrals in Southern Arizona.
