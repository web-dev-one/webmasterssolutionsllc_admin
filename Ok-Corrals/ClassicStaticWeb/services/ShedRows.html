What Are Shed Rows?

Shed rows are versatile structures designed to provide shelter for horses, livestock, and equipment. They are typically built with a series of connected stalls or bays, offering shade, protection from the elements, and storage space. At OK Corrals, we specialize in designing and installing custom shed rows tailored to your specific needs.

Phoenix, AZ, is known for its extreme weather conditions, from scorching summer heat to sudden monsoon storms.
