Premium Custom Horse Shelters

At Ok-Corrals, we understand that every horse owner has unique needs. Our custom horse shelters are designed to provide optimal protection from Arizona's harsh sun, wind, and rain while complementing your property's aesthetics.

Whether you need a simple run-in shed or a more elaborate shelter with additional gates, panels, and accessories, we can create the perfect solution for your horses.

Why Choose Our Custom Shelters?

Sun Protection

Our shelters provide essential shade to protect your horses from Arizona's intense sunlight, reducing heat stress and sunburn.

Weather Resistant

Built to withstand monsoon rains, dust storms, and strong winds while keeping your horses dry and comfortable.

Custom Configurations

Choose from various sizes, roof styles, and additional features like feeders, waterers, and partitions.

Durable Construction

Made with high-quality materials that resist chewing, kicking, and weathering for long-lasting performance.
