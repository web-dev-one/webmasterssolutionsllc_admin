Frequently Asked Questions

How do these compare to traditional barn stalls?

Our shade stalls are 30-50% cooler than enclosed barns in summer while providing equal protection. They're ideal for Arizona's dry climate where airflow is crucial. For fully enclosed options, see our steel barn designs.

Can they withstand monsoon storms?

Yes! All structures are engineered for 70+ mph winds with ground anchors that meet Arizona building codes.

Do you service my area?

We cover all of Southern Arizona including Phoenix, Tucson, Prescott, and surrounding communities. Contact us to confirm service in your location.

What maintenance is required?

Just occasional hosing of roofing (removes dust that reduces reflectivity) and checking bolt tightness after extreme winds.
