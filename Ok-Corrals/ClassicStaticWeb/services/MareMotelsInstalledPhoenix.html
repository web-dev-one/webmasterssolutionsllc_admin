Why Mare Motels are Essential for Horse Breeders

Mare motels are specialized foaling stalls designed to provide a safe, clean, and stress-free environment for mares and their newborn foals. In Phoenix, AZ, where temperatures can fluctuate dramatically, having a well-designed mare motel is crucial for ensuring the health and safety of your horses during the foaling process.

  • Safety: Protects mares and foals from predators and harsh weather conditions.
  • Comfort: Provides a clean, dry, and spacious environment for foaling.
  • Convenience: Allows breeders to monitor mares closely during the foaling process.
